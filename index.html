Speech Therapy Exercises

Speech Therapy Techniques

Tongue Twister Challenges

Try to pronounce tongue twisters quickly and clearly. Get feedback on your speed and accuracy.

Phoneme Practice

Select a phoneme to practice. Focus on sounds like "s," "sh," or "r" to improve articulation.

Minimal Pairs

Differentiate between similar-sounding words like "bat" and "pat" to improve articulation.

Sentence Repetition

Repeat sentences to improve speech fluency and articulation accuracy.

Breathing and Pausing Practice

Practice controlled breathing and pausing during speech for better fluency.

Story Completion

Complete a story by speaking aloud. This helps improve fluency and creativity in speech.

Pitch and Tone Modulation

Practice varying your pitch and tone to match different emotions like happy, sad, or excited.

Syllable Counting

Count the syllables in words or phrases to improve speech rhythm.
